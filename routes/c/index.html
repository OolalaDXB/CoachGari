Online coaching

Live Sessions, Not A PDF
You Never Open

Most online coaching is a file and then silence. This is a standing appointment with someone who can see what you're doing and tell you to fix it — and who follows up when the week goes quiet.

We meet on video

Live, twice a month. I watch you move, correct it in real time, and rewrite the week in front of you.

Built for what you have

A full gym, a set of bands, or a floor and a wall. The plan is written for your kit, not an ideal one.

Food handled with it

Simple habits around the food where you live and what it costs there. No meal plan full of things you can't buy.

Talk to Gari

Sometimes The Session
Is The Conversation

Not every call has to end in a workout. Some people book an hour just to talk it through — what's blocking them, why the last three attempts stopped, what they actually want out of this.

It's the part of coaching I like most, and it works the same whether you're down the road or six time zones away.

About

Zimbabwe To Dubai,
And Back Again

Born in Zimbabwe and raised between Zimbabwe and South Africa, I've spent the last ten years in Dubai as a personal trainer, sports coach and entrepreneur.

I lead coaching programmes, member experience and community activation — the on-the-ground side of building somewhere people want to keep coming back to. Most of the people I coach now are nowhere near me, which suits both of us fine.
