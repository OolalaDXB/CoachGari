How it works

Training And Food,
Handled Together

There's no separate nutrition package, because splitting them never works. What you eat gets sorted alongside the training — simple habits built around the food where you live, your budget and your week. Not a meal plan full of things you can't buy.

We start with a call

What you've tried, what you've got to train with, how much time is real rather than aspirational.

You get a plan you can keep

Built for your kit and your week. If it only works on a perfect week, it isn't a plan.

I notice when you stop

Most people don't need another programme. They need someone who spots the week they went missing.

Sometimes The Session
Is The Conversation

Not every call has to end in a workout. Some people book an hour to talk it through — what they're stuck on, why the last three attempts stopped, what they actually want out of this.

It's the part of coaching I like most, and it works whether you're in Dubai or six time zones away.
